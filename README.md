## Project Description

**HeatHarvest / Proof-of-Growth (2025â€“2026)** is an open-source field project proving that **Bitcoin miner waste-heat can grows real food at scale** through harsh winters, in short-season climates, and far from traditional supply chains. Our near-term goal: **hundreds of pounds of organic purple sweet potatoes**, plus a **crawfish aquaponics** pilot, all warmed by captured Bitcoin mining heat.

By converting **hashrate â†’ harvest**, communities can **extend growing seasons**, **stabilize greenhouse and water temperatures**, and **earn bitcoin**  reallocate to power, tools, and facilities. For remote or underbanked regions, HeatHarvest connects growers directly to the **global, peer-to-peer Bitcoin economy** turning energy into both **nourishment** and **economic freedom**.

This repo publishes open designs, firmware, build guides, logs, and yields so anyone can reproduce results.
